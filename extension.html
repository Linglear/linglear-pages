How to Install & Use Linglear

1. Download & Install

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for “Linglear” or click here. Click Add to Chrome and confirm the installation.

2. Authenticate

Open the extension popup and click Login. A secure AWS Cognito window will appear – sign in with your account to receive an access token.

3. Subscribe

Navigate to the Payment tab of the popup. Click Subscribe to open Stripe’s hosted checkout. After payment, your subscription becomes active via our secure webhook integration【419235211640588†L97-L106】.

4. Enjoy the Features

Start summarising articles or transcribing audio with our AI. The extension keeps you signed in and manages your subscription automatically.

5. Manage Your Plan

Need to update or cancel? Use the Manage Billing button in the Payment tab to access Stripe’s Billing Portal. There you can make changes to your plan or update payment methods.
